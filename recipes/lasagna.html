Lasagna Sauce

Description

Delicious lasagna sauce that I often pair with my veggie lasgna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (29 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles

  1. Heat 1/4 cup oil in large skillet over medium heat
  2. Stir in the chopped onions and bay leaf; cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, takes about 5 minutes.
  3. Add 2 cloves minced garlic, oregano and 2 teaspoons basil; cook and stir for 2 more minutes.
  4. Mix in undrained crushed tomatoes and diced tomatoes.
  5. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 1 hour.
  6. Finally, stir in cinnamon and done.
