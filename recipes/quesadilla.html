Cheese Quesadilla

Description

While, this is only a recipe for a cheese quesadilla, you may add your own fillings. Some examples would be, chicken, pico de gallo, black beans, steak, etc.

Ingredients

  • Mexican cheese blend
  • 10 inch flour tortillas
  • olive oil or butter

Steps

  1. Add 1/2 a tablespoon of olive oil to a large skillet.
  2. Heat tortillas in the skillet on medium high heat. Do this until air pockets form within the tortilla.
  3. Sprinkle a handful of shredded cheese allover the tortilla.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and cover the skillet.
  5. After a minute, check to see if the cheese is melted. If not, put the cover back on and cook for another minute.
  6. Fold your tortilla in half and remove from the skillet.
  7. Lastly, cut your quesadilla in two and serve.
